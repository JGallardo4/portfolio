Ascended Guild

In the MMORPG World of Warcraft , groups of players are called guilds. This website was built for one such guild, with players in various parts of North America.

This site was built for one such guild called Ascended. Through this site, prospective members can see at a glance which positions are available, watch streams from guild members, and learn all about the guild.
